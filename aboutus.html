About MindfulMe

Our goal is to Understand and Address Social Media Addiction

Who Are We?

MindfulMe is a dedicated team passionate about enhancing digital wellness and promoting a balanced, mindful lifestyle.

What we do?

We offer 'MindfulMe,' a digital wellness app designed to help users manage social media usage and improve their mental, physical, and emotional well-being.

How we do it?

By leveraging personalized data analysis, mindfulness practices, and engaging skill-building activities, we empower users to replace habitual social media scrolling with more fulfilling and healthful habits.
